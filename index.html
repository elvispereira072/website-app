Store Owner:

João Silva, founder and owner of Harmonia Musical, is an experienced and enthusiastic musician with more than 20 years of career in the world of music. João brought his passion and knowledge to create a store that serves both beginners and professional musicians. His vision is to provide a space where everyone can find instruments and accessories needed to express their musical creativity.
João believes that music transforms lives and is always available to offer advice and share his experiences with customers.
